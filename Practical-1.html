Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering

The Bharati Vidyapeeth’s College of Engineering (BVCOE) is a private engineering institute established in the year 1999. It has the approval of AICTE and is affiliated to the Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. Presently, the college is running Bachelor of Technology across 5 specializations. The college aims at providing the best technical education under highly qualified & experienced faculties. There are also tremendous career opportunities at this college through campus placements

Library

The College has a big libary that can accomodate more than 150 people. It is a digital library with the facility of WIFI. Library has very rich collections of Books (Print & Electronic) & database on various branches (Applied Science, CSE, IT, ECE, EEE, ICE) of Knowledge. The print and digital holdings at the Library are well-balanced. Almost 53455 collections of print books are housed in the Library. The Library Resource Center provides a venue for study, innovation, and growth, in keeping with the stated goal and the BVCOE’s goal to “support quality education.” The library is solely dedicated to student’s researchers’, and faculty’s academic needs. It carries a wide range of publication in fields such as Computer Science, Information Technology, Mathematics, Physics, Electronics, and Electrical Engineering. In addition, the library includes print subscriptions to approximately 55 national and international periodicals and magazines. The library’s subscribes numerous local, regional, and national newspapers in Hindi, English, and Marathi. With the use of in-house Library automation Software, known as ‘LIB SYS,’ all automated activities are entirely automated.. The library subscribes to be a range of online database to help users and their academic persiuts.IEE (ASPP), Pearson e books collection. Library has been active member of Developing Library Networks (DELNET), National Digital Library of India (NDLI).
